Carol Miller

Republican, 3rd District, West Virginia

Carol Miller is a small business owner, bison farmer, and State Delegate who is running for Congress to bring West Virginia values to Washington DC. Carol is pro-life, pro-Second Amendment, and pro-coal. Carol will work with President Trump to build the wall and end illegal immigration. In the State House, Carol prayed before every vote and she’ll do the same in Congress. EDUCATION: BA, Columbia College CURRENT OFFICE OR OCCUPATION: Small Business Owner, Bison Farmer, and State Delegate. ENDORSEMENTS: Susan B. Anthony List (National Pro-life Group), Winning for Women. FAMILY: Husband of 45 years, Matt Miller; sons and daughters-in-law, Chris and Cassie Miller and Sam and Katie Miller; five grandchildren.
